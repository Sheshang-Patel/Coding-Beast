About

Institute of Technology, Nirma University was established in the year 1995 by the Nirma Education and Research Foundation (NERF). The Institute is the first self-financed engineering college of the State of Gujarat. All the BTech Programmes are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) under Tier-I category. It is one of the most sought after institutes and has been recognised as a leading centre of education in the country. The Institute offers multidisciplinary undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in Engineering and Technology. It has been consistently ranked among the top self-financed engineering colleges of India.

Achievements
  • Rankings in the year 2020 by leading survey magazines like India Today placed the Institute at 15th position among 200 private colleges across the country and at 5th position in the West Zone. Education World has placed the Institute at 2nd Position in the state of Gujarat and at 24th position at national level. The Institute has been home to two Centres of Excellence like Data Science and Robotics and Automation. Department of Civil Engineering has recently developed an indigenous Heavy Structures Laboratory (HSL) to conduct research, consultancy and testing in the field of Structural Engineering. Seven final year students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering bagged the pre-placement offers (PPOs) from Amazon with the CTC of INR 30.00 LPA, and fifteen students of the final year received offers from Oracle with the CTC of INR 11.00 LPA. Students of the Institute grabbed the first prize and a special prize at the Smart India Hackathon (SIH 2020) – an initiative of the MHRD, Government of India. Students of the Department of Instrumentation and Control Engineering are awarded the prestigious ISA Educational Foundation Scholarship Award and ISA PMCD Scholarship Award. The Team CON-SOL-E of the Department were the proud winners of the Mitsubishi Electric Cup this year too; maintaining the winning streak from 2017 to 2020. Three teams from the Department of Chemical Engineering bagged the first, second and third prizes for the Storm Case Challenge “Industrial Problem Solving” competition organized by Thermax Global Private Limited for Chemical Engineering students across India. Team ROBOCON has once again proved its merit and supremacy in the National ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest by being the first Runner Up in the National Championship ROBOCON 2019, conducted at IIT, Delhi. In the recently concluded All-Terrain Vehicle National Level Competition organised by SAE-BAJA, the m-BAJA team brought laurels to the Institute by winning the Best Skill BAJA award for exhibiting exemplary manufacturing skills. The Team e-BAJA also won five awards, including first in Sales, first in CAE, first in acceleration, and first in durability, maintaining the excellent performance in SAE-BAJA in 2019.
